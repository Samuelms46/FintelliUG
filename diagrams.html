System Components:

• Coordinator Agent: Orchestrates all specialized agents using LangGraph
• Three Specialized Agents: Handle social intelligence, competitor analysis, and market sentiment
• Vector Database: Stores and retrieves conversation patterns for similarity matching
• Dashboard: Real-time investor interface with actionable insights
